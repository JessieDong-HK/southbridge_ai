## 一、使命与愿景

### 英文原文

**Vision**:
> "NYU Shanghai exemplifies the highest ideals of contemporary higher education by uniting the intellectual resources of New York University's global network with the multidimensional greatness of China. It guides students toward academic and moral excellence, preparing them for leadership in all walks of life."

**Mission** (教学部分核心):
> "NYU Shanghai aspires to prepare its students for lives of discovery, satisfaction and contribution. They will study with superb teachers who nurture their capacity for original, rigorous, and critical thinking... They will pursue a liberal education in the humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and mathematics. They will immerse themselves in English, the language of international communication. They will master the skills of cross-cultural effectiveness in a community where half are from China and half are from other lands."

**七大价值观**: Curiosity（好奇）、Rigor（严谨）、Integrity（正直）、Respect（尊重）、Harmony（和谐）、Responsibility（尽责）、Deep engagement with all humanity（全面融入人文社会）

### 中文原文

> "上海纽约大学将纽约大学全球教育体系中丰富的智力资源融入灿烂的中国文化，身体力行当代高等教育的最高理想，带领学生获得学业与精神的双重收获。"

## 二、历史与定位

- **2006年**：NYU 海外学习中心首批18位学生抵达华东师大
- **2012年9月**：教育部正式批准——**"世界上第一所中美合作创办的研究型大学"**（the world's first-ever Sino-US research university）
- **2014年**：世纪大道校园启用
- **2020年6月**：创校校长俞立中退休，童世骏接任
- **2022年**：前滩新校园（New Bund Campus）建成

**核心定位**：
- NYU 全球教育体系中第三个授予学位的校园（与纽约、阿布扎比并列）
- "an institution of higher education that is simultaneously American and Chinese"
- 中国第一所中美合办大学

## 四、领导层

### 校长 — 童世骏 (Tong Shijun)

2020年起任第二任校长。华东师大哲学系教授（1994年至今），挪威卑尔根大学哲学博士。师从著名哲学家冯契。翻译哈贝马斯《在事实与规范之间》。挪威科学与文学院外籍院士。

**核心理念**：对话（dialogue）——受哈贝马斯"交往理性"影响。将大学行政管理视为哲学实践。

**金句**：
> "If you want to cultivate really cosmopolitan minds, you must create conditions for real interactions for young minds, young people."

> "Both America and China have strong and rich traditions of education... I think we have a duty to bring our best tradition into the collaboration and try to learn the best part from the other side."

> "Of all the awards I have been given, I am most proud of this title - Best Teacher - because that's the one I received on the basis of the votes of my students."

### 创校校长 — 俞立中 (Yu Lizhong)

"文革"期间在黑龙江农场务农九年，恢复高考后考入华东师大地理系。2012-2020年任创校校长。中国首位开设个人微博的大学校长（170万粉丝），通过微信与每届学生直接联系。

**金句**：
> "Education changed my life."

> "Are you ready to take a different path that has more risk but also more unexpected opportunities?"

> "It was the right time, right place, and right people."

NYU前校长John Sexton评价：
> "Li is one of those rare human beings who are centered in virtue and great values. He had a great vision for education and for the world, one of a humankind that was united for the progress of all."

### 常务副校长（美方校长）— 雷蒙 / Jeffrey S. Lehman

自2012年建校起担任。康奈尔大学第11任校长、密歇根大学法学院前院长、北大国际法学院创院院长。曾获中国"友谊奖"和上海市荣誉市民。

### 教务长 — 吴蓓 / Bei Wu

NYU全球杰出公共卫生学教授，国际公认的老年学领军人物，学术成果超1,000篇。

## 五、校园

**前滩校园（2022年启用）**：浦东新区前滩国际商务区，9层学术大楼，四栋建筑合围，融合现代建筑与古典庭院。总建筑面积约11.4万平方米。600人礼堂、78个教室、5,000平方米图书馆、4,000平方米体育中心。

**宿舍 — 璟耀公寓**：浦东后滩，步行10分钟至地铁站。Wi-Fi、空调、独立卫浴和厨房。室友来自不同国家。
