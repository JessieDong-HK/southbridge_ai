## 五、四大教育使命（官网原文）

1. "Curiosity, passion and the search for truth augmented by advanced research skills"
2. "A liberal arts pedagogy that emphasizes educational breadth, free inquiry and deep engagement"
3. "Pluralism, tolerance and a willingness to engage with and learn from others"
4. "Community action and service that is globally connected and locally grounded"
