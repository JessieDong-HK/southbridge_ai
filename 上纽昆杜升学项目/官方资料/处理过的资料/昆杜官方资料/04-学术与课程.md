## 一、学位与学制

### 双学位

> "Duke Kunshan University students successfully completing the course of study will be conferred both a Duke Kunshan University graduation certificate and diploma officially approved by the MOE as well as a diploma from Duke University. Students will be alumni of both institutions."

- 昆山杜克大学学位证书（中国教育部认证）
- 杜克大学学位证书（SACSCOC 认证）
- 毕业生成为**两所大学的校友**

### 学制

| 项目 | 详情 |
|------|------|
| 总学制 | 4年本科（可在 **6 年内**完成学位要求） |
| 定专业 | **大二**确定专业方向 |
| 换专业 | 可以换，但不建议太晚换 |
| 教学语言 | **全英文** |

*（来源：`about__academics-18b99c579f.json`, `about__academic-calendar-914518be6c.json`）*

### 7周短学期制

DKU 采用独特的 **7 周教学Session**：
> "With its seven-week terms and attention to pedagogy, DKU is uniquely positioned to engage in these best practices."

短学期意味着更高密度的学习、更高的效率，也意味着需要较强的自律和时间管理能力。

## 五、教学理念

### 探究式教学

> "Mastering academic material is the beginning, not the end point, of a Duke Kunshan University education."

> "Through inquiry-based methods of instruction, students will become comfortable approaching complex problems and using their acquired knowledge and experiences to engage deeply with the world."

### 跨学科框架

> "The Duke Kunshan curriculum begins from liberal arts principles and is imbued with the hallmarks of a Duke education blended with Chinese tradition: interdisciplinary approaches, engagement with research questions, problem-based and team-based learning, and opportunities for students to craft individual pathways."

课程特色：
- 跨学科方法
- 以研究问题为驱动
- 基于问题和团队的学习
- 学生定制个人学习路径

## 六、AI 与教学

*（来源：`center-for-teaching-and-learning__faculty-resource-guide-teaching-with-ai-fb4af613e3.json`, `center-for-teaching-and-learning__ai-and-teaching-7c43be81ef.json`）*

DKU 在教学中对 AI 的态度非常前沿：

### DKU 双模式 AI 流畅度框架（2026草案）

> "Beyond the Algorithm: Developing Evaluative Agency in the Age of Artificial Intelligence"

核心理念：不是禁用 AI，而是**培养学生的 AI 评价能力**。

### AI 教学实践

- 鼓励学生与 ChatGPT 进行**深度辩论**
- 用生成式 AI 作为**导师**帮学生理解复杂科学概念
- 教师被鼓励：(1) 提升 AI 素养 (2) 提醒学生原创思考的价值 (3) 坦诚讨论 AI 的局限性

> 这不是政策文件，而是一套在生成式 AI 时代有效教学的一般建议。

### 为什么 DKU 适合探索 AI 教育

> "With its seven-week terms and attention to pedagogy, DKU is uniquely positioned to engage in these best practices... DKU faculty will need to rely on pedagogical and assessment practices that best support learning: active engagement, authentic assessment, delivered in our immersive classes."
